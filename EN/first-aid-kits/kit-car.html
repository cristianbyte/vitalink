Basic first aid kit
for car

Antiseptics

When we talk about antiseptics, we are talking about products that are applied on the skin to avoid contamination and infection by germs.

  • ✔ An example of these solutions is found in hydrogen peroxide, alcohol ethyl, chlorhexidine or tincture of iodine.
  • ✔ Alcohol, in turn, will be good to disinfect metal utensils, such as tweezers, used to make cures.

Cotton

A classic that cannot be missing in our biker's first aid kit.

  • ✔ Likewise, improvised between layers of gauze will act as a dressing.
  • ✔ Among other uses, cotton is used to clean the contour of wounds or moisten antiseptics to disinfect non-open areas of the skin.

Gauzes

There are different types. A first aid kit usually includes sterile gases, whose purpose is to absorb liquids and secretions, stop bleeding or clean and protect wounds.

  • ✔ One of the advantages of this product is that it does not stick to wounds.
  • ✔ And by ensuring optimal protection, it contributes to healing.

Disposable latex gloves

To heal a wound, the first step has to be washing your hands with soap or water. And another option is to use disinfectant wipes. However, whenever possible, the Experts recommend putting on disposable latex gloves during first responder interventions. aids.
